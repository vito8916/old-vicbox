PortraitsBox

What is this?
Portraits box is a minimalist photo gallery that shows different albums and photographic styles such as portraits, landscapes, nature and animals. But the essence of PortraitsBox is to show portraits of people and tell their story through photography.
The simplicity of this gallery makes it a pleasant experience to see these beautiful photographs or read a beautiful story of the photo you are seeing.

SimplyPaper - Wallpaper

SimplyPaper is an app that offers you the best wallpapers for your smartphone with a fabulous innovative design and easy to use.
The images adapt to any screen size of smartphones regardless of their resolution or pixel accuracy per inch (dpi) so you don't miss a single pixel of your favorite image.
